# Code of Conduct

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

Short version: be respectful, give people the benefit of the doubt,
focus on what's best for the project, and don't be the reason someone
stops contributing.

Reports of unacceptable behaviour can be sent to **berengamble@gmail.com**.
All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
